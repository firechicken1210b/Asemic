《Let us walk to find out what today means / 把喜歡的果子、水、花、葉子帶給身邊的你》

png , 15000 x 3000 , 2023

這次的創作動機起於我從薄伽梵歌中得到表達感謝的靈感，覺得其實我需要的一直都以各種的面貌存在我的生活中，希望能分享我遇到的開心給身邊的人，如同生活中的食物、水、花、葉子，都帶給我許多的滿足與感激。我有時會想，會不會那些我以為開心的事，其實都很奇怪，但我還是想跟你們分享，也謝謝你們聽我分享也都很開心。
I was inspired by the Bhagavad Gita to express my gratitude and felt that what I needed was always present in my life in various forms, and I wanted to share the happiness I found with those around me, just as the food, water, flowers and leaves in my life bring me so much satisfaction and gratitude. I sometimes wonder if the things I think are happy are actually strange, but I still want to share them with you, and thank you for sharing happiness together.

製作的流程是先使用電繪板繪製符號（平常是手繪於便條紙），然後使用Stable Diffusion產生果、水、花、葉的素材，並挑選合適的素材訓練LoRA，共訓練出四個LoRA。使用sd的img2img和訓練出來的LoRA生成畫面，最後決定採用由左至右共有五個畫面的作品構圖，最左邊是我繪製的符號原圖，然後依序是由AI所聯想的果、水、花、葉，透過這個構圖呈現與AI共同創作的過程。
The creative process involved first using a digital drawing tablet to create Asemic (usually hand-drawn on sticky notes), then using Stable Diffusion to generate materials for fruits, water, flowers, and leaves, and selecting suitable materials to train LoRA. Four LoRAs were trained in total. By the img2img function of SD and the trained LoRA were used to generate the final images. The composition of the artwork consists of five frames from left to right. The leftmost frame is the original one drawn by me, followed by fruits, water, flowers, and leaves that were generated by AI. Through the appreciation process, I also feel the joy of co-creation with AI.

最初我是想讓AI幫我把所有東西都看成符號的，那可能也是一種新的繪畫方式可以嘗試，我也覺得與AI的合作中，讓手作成為凸顯的主軸可能比較有溫度，又或者只是我喜歡畫符號，我只是想保留我喜歡的事給自己作。
Originally, I wanted AI to help me see everything as Asemic, which could be a new way of drawing to explore. However, in my collaboration with AI, I feel that emphasizing the handmade aspect is more warm and humanity. Perhaps it's just because I enjoy drawing Asemic, and I simply want to keep doing what I love.

在與AI的合作中，我喜歡畫符號，而AI擅長想像，我們可以各自做自己喜歡或擅長的事。最終產出的作品給我的感覺，像是生活中的事物揭示了它們真正的名字，而這些名字代表了我們與事物在共同生活中累積出來的經歷。
In collaboration with AI, I enjoy drawing Asemic while AI excels at imagination, and we can each do what we like or are good at. The final artwork gives me the feeling that the things in life reveal their true names, and those names represent the experiences we accumulate together with them throughout our lives.
